June ??, 2023

Update 3.5 is here with the biggest, baddest race in all of stock car racing: the Atlas City 600. Take on 400 laps at the 1.5 mile oval for a total of 600 miles filled with drafting, strategy, and the looming threat of The Big One.

Additions

Atlas City Superspeedway

  • Tame the high-banked curves of this 1.5 mile superspeedway!
  • Participate in Left Turn Legend events here in all four of the original series: Compact Clash, Muscle Stock Showdown, Legends Cup, and Run What Ya Brung
  • Take on the Atlas City 600, a 400-lap, 600-mile showdown of 40 of the best Legends Cup racers in the world!
  • Restrictor plate racing requires clever drafting to win

Fuel/Tire Wear and Pit Stops

  • Manage your fuel and tires in all Left Turn Legends events
  • Make your way down pit lane for live stops
  • Speeding penalties for going too fast down pit road
  • Choose how much fuel and tires to take to win on strategy
  • 40 pit stalls in Atlas City Superspeedway
  • New pit road added to Creekfield Raceway

Drafting

  • Tuck in behind an opponent to make use of their draft, or make a strategic block to get a push!
  • Side draft an opponent to slow them down while slingshotting yourself out front
  • Slide behind a large group of cars to get the biggest runs

Cautions

  • Serious wrecks will bring out the yellow flag and bunch the field back up for a restart
  • Free pass for the highest placed car not on the lead lap
  • Wave around if all lead lap cars pit under caution
  • Green-white-checkered overtime if restarting with less than 2 to go
  • Cautions will not fly after the leader takes the white flag

Creekfield Raceway Changes

  • Added pit road
  • Legends Cup races can now have up to 24 cars
  • Increased AI difficulty

Bug Fixes:

  • Fix save fidelity issues on iOS
  • Fix lag on statistics screen
  • Fix semitransparent decals causing inconsistent paint colors
  • Fix missing barriers in the race "Across the Bridge"
  • Fix discoloration around headlights of Plastico, t8, and BXR
  • Fix some races allowing the player to go out of bounds via newer areas of the map
  • Fix missing headlight glare from Bullet (1967), Hornet (2016), and Hornet (1987) in Left Turn Legend events
  • Fix player being able to shift gears while paused with manual transmission and controller
  • Fix Title Defense event location changing after participating in an unrelated race
  • Removed ball car
