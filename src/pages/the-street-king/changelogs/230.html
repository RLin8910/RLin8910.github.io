December 29, 2020

Update 2.3 is here with new camera options.

Additions

  • Added 2 new dynamic camera options!
  • Added orbit camera when driving around.
  • Added driver customization - change the color of your driver's racing suit!
  • Added music display - when songs change, the name of the current song will appear along with an option to skip it.

Changes

  • Changed some controls on controller in order to accomodate the new orbit camera:
  • The orbit camera can be moved with the right joystick
  • Shifting up and down with manual transmission now uses the bumpers instead of the right stick
  • The left bumper can no longer be used to look back - use the Y button instead.
  • In the Map and Screenshot screens, the camera is now moved with the right stick and zoomed in and out with the left stick

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed misplaced palm tree appearing between two parking garages
  • Fixed floating palm trees along the beach
  • Fixed bumper cam showing objects that aren't suppposed to be shown
  • Fixed some road signs not reacting to collisions on Freedom St.
  • Fixed player escaped message appearing online after a player was busted
  • Fixed bug where the same song would play multiple times in a row in freeroam
  • Fixed bug where wrecking after finishing a race in online multiplayer could cause issues with the wrong menus showing up
  • Fixed a roadblock spawning bug in the highway exits near the Moore Bridge
