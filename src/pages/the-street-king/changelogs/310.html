June 26, 2022

Version 3.1 adds some cool features and fixes some bugs.

Additions

Improved Police AI

  • Police cars have improved pathfinding
  • Police will try to follow the player through more complex routes
  • It is no longer possible to cheese police chases by hiding in a corner

New Fixed Headlight Conversions

  • Rotor2
  • Fuoco
  • OVR8
  • Acrobat
  • Inferno

Uncolored Brand Decals

  • All brand decals now have uncolored variants
  • These decals are white by default but can be colored by the player

Changes

  • Daily rewards can no longer be claimed during an online multiplayer session
  • It is now much easier to resize buttons in the controls customization screens

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed issue with collision mesh at the beginning of the Atlas Bridge turnpike
  • Fixed floating street sign on the intersection of Harolds Road and Summit Road
  • Fixed traffic cars in online multiplayer making sudden uturns
  • Removed ball car
