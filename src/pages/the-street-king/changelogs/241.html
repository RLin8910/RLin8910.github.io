March 27, 2021

Update 2.41 prevents inexperienced players from joining online multiplayer.

Additions

  • Multiplayer and the track are now locked until the first singleplayer race is completed
  • Added "Singleplayer Races Completed" and "Current Drag Ranking" to the player statistics screen
  • The GPS will now automatically route to the next singleplayer race if the player has not manually added a marker

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed a critical bug where the game would softlock upon returning to the garage in certain circumstances
  • Fixed a bug where the return to garage and reset vehicle buttons would remain disabled even after resuming the game
  • Fixed a bug where traffic would stop spawning or spawn in decreased amounts
  • Fixed bug where some sounds could be heard in loading screens
  • Fixed bug where other music would briefly be heard before the tutorial loaded in
  • Fixed bug where the player car would be briefly shown in another location before the tutorial began
