October 29, 2021

Update 2.8 introduces the Nitro Nights tournament!

Additions

Nitro Nights Tournament

  • Participate in the firstever Nitro Nights online multiplayer tournament!
  • Race for rewards the winner will get a month of Discord Nitro!
  • Nitro Nights decals can now be found in the modshop

Custom Multiplayer Room Settings

  • Private rooms can now be customized!
  • Toggle traffic
  • Toggle police
  • Change time of day
  • Change weather

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed incorrect reflection map on Maddie's Darlington
  • Fixed bad roadblock spawn position in the race "Escape from the Canyons"
  • Fixed incorrect button placement in the drag strips results screen in the test track
  • Fixed bug where gearbox data would not be properly transferred in online multiplayer
  • Fixed incorrect gearing on racer Plasticos
