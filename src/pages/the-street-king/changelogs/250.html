Version 2.5 is here, and it's the biggest update The Street King has ever seen!

Major Additions

  • Freeroam Racers race against randomly generated racers along your own custom race routes, or cruise alongside them in the open world!
  • Title Defense defend the crown against allnew opponents and old enemies! Win as many races as you can in a row.
  • Car Vote #2 Winners Two allnew midengined beasts join the car list!

Minor Additions

  • Added title defense requirement for 100%ing the game
  • Added 3 new achievements Roaming Around, Long Live the King, and IronFisted Tyrant
  • Added 1 new leaderboard Title Defense Record
  • Added 12 new police officer names
  • Added penitentiary bus

Changes

  • Police cars no longer immediately despawn when their target escapes
  • Drag race locations now spawn even if drag races are locked

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed bug where Bea's tire skidmark and smoke effects failed to show up in the opening tutorial cutscene
  • Fixed bug where the "cars owned" statistic would only show up to 35 cars
  • Fixed a minor typo in the upgrade guide
