July 27, 2021

Update 2.63 fixes a few minor bugs.

Changes

  • Changed V10 sounds to make them unique from the Flat-10 engine
  • Reduced AI difficulty in "Crash the Party"
  • Multiplayer race requests are now automatically declined if the players are too far apart

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed bug where starting a race in multiplayer would not repair your car's windows or hood
  • Fixed bug where the BXR's roof scoops would fail to show up during gameplay
  • Fixed police cars being in the wrong spot in "Cop Crasher" if the cutscene is skipped
  • Fixed bug where it was possible to pause the game while restarting a race
  • Fixed saturation and brightness bar colors not updating sometimes when tinting windows
  • Fixed saturation and brightness bar colors not updating sometimes when changing neon colors
  • Fixed saturation and brightness bar colors not updating sometimes when changing pearlescent color
  • Fixed saturation and brightness bar colors never updating when changing decal color
  • Fixed roadblocks spawning in the rockslide area of the Rocky Summit
  • Fixed bug where nitro would not be refilled when starting a multiplayer race
