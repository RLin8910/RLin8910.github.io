September 10, 2020

Version 2.03 has been released with a few small changes.

Additions

  • Added social media links (Discord, YouTube, my website) and sharing links (Facebook, Twitter) to the main menu
  • Added support for highquality screenshots (they are now saved as .png files at native device resolution!)

Changes

  • White flash in menu transitions has been changed to a black fadeout to avoid the harsh effect
  • Some minor rebalancing on cars
  • Slight changes in the models on the widebody kits of some cars
  • Reduced NPC car update tickrate in online to lower the amount of network traffic

Bug Fixes

  • (Hopefully) fixed the pink windows and messed up hood bug
  • Fixed errors that would occur if you joined multiplayer and immediately went back to the garage
  • Fixed getting kicked out of multiplayer for AFK immediately upon joining
  • Fixed one car having the wrong stock engine type
  • Fixed bug where quitting and rejoining the game in singleplayer would allow you to reset your car to escape the cops
