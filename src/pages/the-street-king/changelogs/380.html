May 9, 2024

The Street King update 3.8 is here with the Final Car Vote Winner and lots of cool features!

Additions

Le Mans (2017)
  • The 2017 Le Mans is here with active aero, a twin-turbocharged V6, and blistering performance.
Wheel Size and Color Customization
  • Change the width, diameter, and rim size of front and rear wheels independently
  • Change rim colors
Active Aero
  • The Terremoto, GT3, and Le Mans (2017) now have active aero bits which move depending on your driving
Flag Decals
  • Flag decals for 30 countries are now available in the livery editor
Miscellaneous Additions
  • Added money and time of day display in the pause menu
  • Added model years to car names in multiplayer chat to allow differentiation
  • Added boss car options to multiplayer chat

Changes

  • Boss races can now be restarted on any difficulty
  • Renamed "Warhead" to "Patriot Warhead" in multiplayer chat
  • Improved camera positioning for customizing rims

Bug Fixes

  • Removed ball car
