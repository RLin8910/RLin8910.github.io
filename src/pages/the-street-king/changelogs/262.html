July 20, 2021

Update 2.62 fixes a few minor bugs.

Changes

  • Starting a multiplayer race will now repair your car and refill your nitro

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed bug where center of mass of traffic cars would be incorrect, which could cause to strange behavior
  • Fixed bug that could cause some scrolling menus to disappear
  • Fixed incorrect material on Bullets that spawn at drag races
  • Fixed bug where wrecked police cars could be "revived" and start a chase again
