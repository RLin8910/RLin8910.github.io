May 7, 2020

The Drag Race Update is here! Version 1.8 of The Street King has arrived with some amazing new features.

Additions

Drag Races

  • Take on 100 racers in quarter-mile drag races.
  • Perfect your shifts and launch your car with absolute precision to win!

Police Raids

  • Cops will raid drag races
  • Higher-profile races will have a higher chance of a raid happening.
