October 23, 2020

Update 2.1 is here with all-new achievements, leaderboards, and cloud saves!

Additions

  • Added Google Play Games integration, complete with cloud saves, achievements, and leaderboards!
  • Save data backup and restore
  • Over 30 new achievements
  • Added speed cameras, with 10 different locations to set records for!
  • Added profile tab to the main menu, where you can view your gameplay statistics

Changes

  • Changed garage icon in the minimap
  • Moved tutorial to "more" section of the main menu
  • Reorganized some things in the main menu
  • Added the upgrade guide to the main menu
  • Changed the app icon

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed camber displaying incorrectly in some cars in the garage
  • Fixed very minor texturing issue with traffic vans
  • Fixed SWAT vans having incorrect rims
  • Fixed race rewards sometimes being off by $1 due to rounding issues
  • Fixed a bug where wrecking the cop in the tutorial would cause singleplayer events to reappear on the map
  • Fixed a bug where GPS waypoints could be placed in the tutorial
  • Fixed bug where a multiplayer race could still be finished even after the car wrecked
  • Fixed bug where a multiplayer race could still be finished even after the player was arrested
  • Fixed a bug where the warning screen for selecting a car of a different tier in singleplayer would display the wrong tier
  • Fixed bug where resetting the vehicle allowed it to move out of bounds in races or inside of traffic cars
  • Fixed roadblock police cars not saving when exiting the app and returning during the race "Venom"
  • Fixed minor memory leak that could occur on one of the police spawners in the race "The Street King"
  • Fixed a bug where exiting the app during the race, opening the race up again, quitting a second time without unpausing, and then returning to the race again would cause race progress to be lost
  • Fixed the drag racer "Danica" having the wrong driver profile picture
