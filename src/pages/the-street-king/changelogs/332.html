January 23, 2023

Version 3.32 adds the ability to revert car customizations and upgrades to stock.

Additions

  • Revert visual customizations to stock
  • Revert performance mods and tuning to stock

Bug Fixes:

  • Fixed an issue which could cause corrupted save data on iOS
  • Fixed some web links being incorrect on iOS
  • Fixed unauthorized players having the official developer tag in online multiplayer
  • Removed ball car
