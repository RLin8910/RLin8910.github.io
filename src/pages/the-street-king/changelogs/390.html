The Street King update 3.9 is here with new features and cars.

Additions

Records
  • Compare your Title Defense, Speed Camera, and race results with other players and view your best results through records!

Other

Bug Fixes

  • Fix materials mismatch on side intakes and spoiler of Tormento in the title screen
  • Fix minor camera bug on race results screen
  • Removed ball car
