Update 2.7 is here to celebrate 3 years of The Street King!

It's been 3 years since The Street King was first published, and to celebrate this, 3 new cars and tons of other new features have arrived!

New Cars

4R V10 Premium

  • As the firstplace winner of the Car Vote, the 4R V10 Premium brings a snarling V10 to the table with AWD and German precision.

Terremoto

  • The 4R V10 Premium's frightening Italian cousin arrives with the same heart and soul under a more aggressive skin.

Monaco F8

  • Is it Australian? Is it American? Who cares, it's an untameable V8 beast!

New Features

Multiplayer Quick Chat

  • Chat in Online Multiplayer with predefined words and phrases
  • Includes locations, cars, actions, and more!

Multiplayer Rooms Overhaul

  • Create private rooms
  • Join public or private rooms using a unique room code

Decal Editor

  • Added full livery imports/exports
  • Added mirror decal button
  • Increased decal limit to 40

Balancing Changes

  • Reduced the difficulty of later races on King and Hard mode
  • Reduced the difficulty of the first few races on all difficulty levels

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed issue where the Toro's wheels could "sink" into the ground
  • Fixed issue where Bullets in drag races would not have the correct brake light material
  • Fixed decal duplicate button having the wrong color when disabled
  • Fixed incorrect carbon fiber hood being used on the 1967 Bullet
  • Fixed transparency issues on the showroom TV screens
  • Fixed excessive traffic car honking when far away from the player
  • Fixed incorrect steering wheel placement in the Rotor2 when in the garage or showroom
  • Fixed police cars respawning in improper locations
  • Fixed bug that allowed nitrous to be used when exiting the garage
  • Fixed reflections in buildings sometimes being incorrect when first loading into a multiplayer room
  • Fixed small delay after several seconds when first loading into a multiplayer room
