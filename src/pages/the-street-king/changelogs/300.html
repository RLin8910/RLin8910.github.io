March 25, 2022

Update 3.0 is here, and it brings some awesome additions!

Additions

iOS Release

  • The Street King is now available on iOS!
  • All features from the Android version have been ported over
  • GameCenter achievements and leaderboards are used instead of Google Play Games
  • iOS and Android players can play online multiplayer together!

New Cars

  • Rotor2 GT: The return of the highrevving rotary!
  • MT6: The Japanese supercar killer
  • Tourer Remake: The 1994 Tourer has been remade from scratch, with new textures and models!

Multiplayer Race Hosting

  • Races have been completely revamped
  • Players can now host races with up to 5 participants
  • Ghost mode
  • Underdog boost
  • Drag physics

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed neon becoming misaligned when switching from Ultra or High graphics to Medium or lower
  • Fixed multiplayer getting stuck connecting when trying to reconnect after failing to join
  • Fixed "Email Support" button in multiplayer screen failing to launch email app on some devices
  • Fixed duplicate shadows spawning under cars
