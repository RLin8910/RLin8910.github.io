March 19, 2023

Version 3.4 is here with the Creekfield 500! This update combines the worlds of Left Turn Legend and The Street King to bring oval circuit stock car racing to The Street King!

Additions

Creekfield Raceway

  • Race in sanctioned events in 4 different leagues on the 1/4 mile dirt oval!
  • Compact Clash: duke it out in cheap, FWD compacts
  • Muscle Stock Showdown: battle for victory in V8-powered muscle cars
  • Legends Cup: race in purpose-built Legends Cup racecars
  • Run What You Brung: no restrictions, drive any car in multi-class races!

Legends Cup Cars

  • 4 new Left Turn Legend cars make their way into the game!
  • Patriot Warhead
  • Hornet SCX
  • Bullet RT760
  • Crown SAD

New Achievements

  • Left Turn Legend: win a race in every Left Turn Legend league
  • The Creekfield 500: win the full 500-lap Creekfield Legends Cup race

Bug Fixes

  • Fix wrong file path being used for some in-game data
  • Removed ball car
