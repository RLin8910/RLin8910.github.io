January 22, 2024

It's a road course! It's an oval! It's...a roval. Update 3.7 is here with the Atlas City Roval 500!

Additions

Atlas City Roval 500
  • Take on 500 kilometers, or 140 laps, on this crazy combination of road course and oval
  • Manage your tire and fuel strategy to drive your way into victory lane

Changes

  • Left Turn Legend AI racers now enter pit lane after completing the race
  • Left Turn Legend AI racers will no longer pit on the last lap
  • Increase tire wear from using handbrake or skidding in Left Turn Legend races
  • Adjust rumblestrips in Atlas City Superspeedway infield
  • Adjust hitboxes of Left Turn Legend cars
  • Adjust braking zone for AI racers entering pit road in Atlas City Superspeedway
  • Adjust tire wear in Left Turn Legend races
  • Reduced frequency of cautions in Left Turn Legend Legends Cup races

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed Competitor incorrectly using widebody hitbox in Left Turn Legend races
  • Fixed Left Turn Legend cars having the same hitbox in Left Turn Legend races
  • Fixed player vehicle not being repaired during Left Turn Legend restart cutscene when pitting under caution
  • Fixed vehicle grip characteristics changing after pitting in Left Turn Legend races
  • Removed ball car
