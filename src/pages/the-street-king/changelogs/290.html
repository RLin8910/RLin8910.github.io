January 23, 2022

Update 2.9 is here with new cars and content!

Additions

Warrigal

  • The Monaco F8 is back - wait, we meant the back is missing, and in its place is a truck bed!

SC8C

  • The ultimate supercar from before the 2010s, powered by 8 supercharged cylinders of pure adrenaline

Konung Reborn

  • The Konung has been remade entirely from scratch
  • New bodykits and customization options!

Supercharger Upgrades

  • All engines can now be supercharged!
  • Some engines perform better with superchargers than turbos, and viceversa
  • Supercharged engines will have a distinct whine
  • Stock turbos and superchargers can be fully removed

Free Camera

  • Explore the world in Screenshot mode with an allnew free camera!
  • Field of view adjustments, free movement, roll, and 360 degree rotation

Controller Support Enhancements

  • Dualshock controllers and Nintendo Switch Pro controllers are now officially supported!
  • Button icons are now correct for the controller type

Changes

  • Car Performance Balancing
  • Car performance when upgraded has been rebalanced.

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed Dave spamming handbrake when driving
  • Fixed floating sign in the canyons
  • Fixed screenshot mode damage toggle not repairing damaged hoods sometimes
  • Fixed neon areas on repair shops not casting shadows
  • Fixed parked cars spawning intersecting each other
  • Fixed police cars getting stuck out of bounds at railroad crossings
  • Fixed some misplaced trees in the mountains
  • Fixed minimap line issues in the race "Tofu Touge"
  • Fixed potential issue that could cause engine sounds to stop
  • Fixed incorrect background scaling on quality settings in the main menu
  • Fixed incorrect parts being selected in the upgrade menus
  • Fixed bug which allowed engines with invalid sizes to be installed

Known Bugs

  • These bugs will be fixed in the next patch there is no need to report them.
  • Dynamic camera will sometimes "snap" into position when near walls
  • Some text in some menus is missing
