Version 2.6 of The Street King is here with some cool new features!

Additions

Car Logos

  • Added brand new logos! Now, every car has a name and stylized logo.
  • Decals for all car logos have been added to the decal editor

Daily Rewards

  • Added daily rewards!
  • Added notification for when a reward is ready to be collected
  • Added notification for 2 hours before a rewards streak expires

Quality of Life

  • Added support for devices with notches and cutouts in the screen
  • Added option for framerate limiting to 15, 30, and 60 FPS
  • Added support for 90 fps limit and unlocked framerate (90+ FPS) on supported devices
  • Added support for Android dynamic app icon

Changes

Multiplayer Changes

  • Multiplayer races now earn money!
  • Quitting in the middle of a race now counts as a loss, and repeatedly quitting races can be penalized
  • Jumping to the garage is no longer allowed in the middle of multiplayer races
  • Removed concrete barrier roadblocks from multiplayer

Other Changes

  • Reduced freeroam racer spawn rate

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed roaming racers being able to drive when wrecked
  • Fixed bug where getting arrested and quitting before respawning at a police station would cause traffic to fail to spawn
  • Fixed bug where it was possible to pause during the black screen before a race started
  • Fixed camera misalignment in the beginning cutscene of "The American Way"
  • Fixed bug where freeroam racers would "fly"
  • Fixed bug where the lowpoly version of the world in the minimap and the background could sometimes turn black if shadows are enabled
  • Fixed bug where leaving multiplayer while your opponent is mapping the race route would softlock your opponent's game
  • Fixed camera bug that could happen in online multiplayer when returning to the garage while in a race
