June 29, 2024

Left Turn Legend Major Update 1.4.0 Released

INTRODUCING WINDYCART - version 1.4.0 is here to celebrate 100 years of open-wheeled Windycart racing!

Additions

10 new Cars
  • 9 new Windycart racecars
  • Stinger MX
10 new Challenges
  • New challenges in all gamemodes

Changes

  • Reduce frequency of ads in challenges
  • Reduce size of ice cube around frozen cars
  • Crates can no longer be "cheated" using cloud save restores

Bug Fixes

  • Fix rendering issues with color sliders
  • Fix bug with remote configurations
