Time really does fly! Exactly two years ago, on New Year's Day 2021, I uploaded my first devlog for Left Turn Legend. Today, I'm releasing Devlog #9!

I'm getting to the point where the release of Left Turn Legend is finally in sight. Development is starting to slow down as I'm devoting more time to cleaning up bugs and functionality as opposed to adding new features. While there's still a lot of work to do, I'm hoping I'll be able to get the game out before July 2023.

In the mean time, check out Devlog #9, and while you're at it, take a look at Devlog #1 and Devlog #7 from the start and 1-year mark!
