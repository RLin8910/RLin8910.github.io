I managed to just barely catch the last day of Monterey Car Week. It was a bit of a drive, but a lot of fun! This post isn't super detailed - it's more of a glorified pic dump than anything else.

Me and the Cars

Bugattis...

...and Benzes

Quick Lunch Break

Before continuing my tour of Pebble Beach, I had some food and enjoyed the landscape.

From the Past...

Monterey featured a ton of incredibly well-maintained classic cars - it was incredible seeing all of the history. It felt surreal seeing these extremely rare, million-dollar vehicles that I'd only ever seen on the internet before sit right in front of me.

...to the Future!

The concept lawn debuted some new cars and concepts to the world - it was the first time the world, let alone I, had seen many of these vehicles.

The new M5 is... big-boned.

There were quite a few classic Porsche "concepts" - for some reason, it seems to be all the rage to EV-swap classic 911s.
