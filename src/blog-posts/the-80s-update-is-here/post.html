After several months of work, I've finished the '80s Update and released it! Download: Android iOS Update Trailer

Changelog:

Additions:

  • Five iconic cars from the 1980s have come to The Street King. Check them out!
  • All-new fixtures have been added to the beach, including a Ferris wheel and park!
  • 6 new synthwave songs have been added to the game's sountrack.
  • Added the eighties retro photo filter to screenshot mode
  • Added carbon fiber hoods for all cars

Changes:

  • Minor changes to the order that cars appear in in the garage and showroom
  • Changes to the AI racers in singleplayer in order to accomodate the new '80s cars.
  • Widened highway exit ramps in the countryside
  • You can now watch an ad to get any visual customization for free, regardless of price.
  • Added more in-depth data privacy settings
  • Your in-game cash is now displayed when purchasing neon.
  • Improved data usage of decal images when joining online multiplayer

Fixes:

  • Fixed parallel parked cars facing the wrong direction
  • Fixed controller input not working when the game is asking for internal storage read/write access
  • Fixed local police units having the wrong paint job
  • Fixed a bug where police cars could duplicate in certain conditions in online multiplayer
  • Fixed a bug where police officers would sometimes have the wrong department icon online
  • Fixed roadblock elements spawning in strange spots in the highway near the Moore Bridge
  • Fixed roadblocks spawning over train tracks and causing issues with the train's physics
  • Fixed some incorrect tree placements in the countryside
  • Fixed the "What's New" screen and the "Data Privacy" screen showing twice when starting the game for the first and second times
  • Fixed the GPS route not being smooth across some corners in certain races
