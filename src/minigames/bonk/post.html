Overview

Bonk A fighting game with only one goal: bonk your opponent's head!

Controls

WASD

  • Jump or fall: w, s
  • Move: a, d

Arrows

  • Jump or fall: up, down
  • Move: left, right

Controller 1

  • Jump or fall: primary button
  • Move: joystick

Controller 2

  • Jump or fall: primary button
  • Move: joystick
